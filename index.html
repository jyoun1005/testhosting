What Is Protein?
If you have ever been on a diet or spoken to somebody about nutrition, then you have surely heard the word protein before. While many people know that protein is important, they may not know what it actually is. The proteins that we eat are made up of different amino acidsâ€”the building blocks of protein. These amino acids are what the body uses for various functions, from forming certain hormones to building specific proteins within the body. So, when the body digests the whole protein that we get from our juicy hamburger or from a hearty lentil soup, the amino acids that make up that protein are absorbed and used by cells for a multitude of functions.
